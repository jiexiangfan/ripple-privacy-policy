# Ripple – Data Deletion Request

This page is for users of the **Ripple** app who want to request deletion of their data.

---

## How to request data deletion

1. [Open a new GitHub Issue](https://github.com/jiexiangfan/ripple-privacy-policy/issues/new) in this repository.
2. Use the title: **Data Deletion Request – [your registered email or username]**
3. In the body, include:
    - The email address or username associated with your Ripple account
    - Confirmation that you want your data deleted

The project team will process your request and confirm deletion within **14 days**.

---

## What data will be deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, the following will be permanently removed:

- Your account and login credentials
- Any content you submitted inside the app
- Usage data associated with your account

## What data may be retained

Some data may be retained for a limited period after deletion for the following reasons:

| Data type                              | Retention period | Reason                                     |
| -------------------------------------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Anonymised usage statistics            | Up to 12 months  | University project evaluation and research |
| Records of the deletion request itself | Up to 12 months  | Compliance and audit trail                 |

Retained data will not be used to identify you personally.
